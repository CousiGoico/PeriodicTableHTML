

==========================
        ATTRIBUTES
==========================

GLOBAL ATTRIBUTES

    - accesskey: shortcut key to put focus in the control.
    - class: class of styles.
    - contenteditable: the control is editable or not.
    - dir: direction the text. Possible values: auto, ltr (left to right), rtl -> (right to left).
    - draggable: the control is draggable or not.
    - hidden: indicate if the control should be rendered.
    - id: unique identifier in the DOM.
    - lang: language of the control.
    - spellcheck: should be check his grammar and spelling.
    - style: attributes of styles.
    - tabindex: tabbing order.
    - title: tooltip.
    - translate: the content of control should be translated.
    - data-*: to save private data.
